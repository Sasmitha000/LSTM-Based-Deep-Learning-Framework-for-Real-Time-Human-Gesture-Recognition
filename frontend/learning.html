Thanks

The "Thanks" sign is performed by bringing your dominant hand to your chin and moving it forward and slightly downward in a graceful motion.

  1. Hold your dominant hand flat with fingers together
  2. Touch your fingertips to your chin
  3. Move your hand forward and slightly down
  4. Keep your palm facing inward throughout
  5. Perform the motion smoothly and naturally
🤟

I Love You

The "I Love You" sign combines the letters I, L, and Y into a single handshape. Extend your thumb, index finger, and pinky while curling your middle and ring fingers down.

  1. Start with your hand in a fist
  2. Extend your thumb outward
  3. Extend your index finger upward
  4. Extend your pinky finger outward
  5. Keep middle and ring fingers curled down
  6. Hold the position facing the camera

💡 Tips for Better Detection

💡 Good Lighting

Make sure your hands are well lit. Avoid strong backlighting from windows.

📏 Distance

Keep your hand about 30-60cm from the camera for best results.

🎯 Clear Background

A plain background helps MediaPipe detect landmarks more accurately.

⏱ Hold Steady

Hold each gesture for at least 1-2 seconds for reliable detection.
